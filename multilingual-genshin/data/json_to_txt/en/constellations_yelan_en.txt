Enter the Plotters
<color=#FFD780FF>Lingering Lifeline</color> gains 1 additional charge.
Taking All Comers
When <color=#FFD780FF>Exquisite Throw</color> conducts a coordinated attack, it will fire an additional water arrow that will deal 14% of Yelan's Max HP as Hydro DMG. This effect can trigger once every 1.8s.
Beware the Trickster's Dice
Increases the Level of <color=#FFD780FF>Depth-Clarion Dice</color> by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Bait-and-Switch
Increases all party members' Max HP by 10% for 25s for every opponent marked by <color=#FFD780FF>Lifeline</color> when the Lifeline explodes. A maximum increase of 40% Max HP can be attained in this manner.
Dealer's Sleight
Increases the Level of <color=#FFD780FF>Lingering Lifeline</color> by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Winner Takes All
After using <color=#FFD780FF>Depth-Clarion Dice</color>, Yelan will enter the Mastermind state. In this state, all of Yelan's Normal Attacks will be special Breakthrough Barbs. These Breakthrough Barbs will have similar abilities to normal ones and the DMG dealt will be considered Charged Attack DMG, dealing 156% of a normal Breakthrough Barb's DMG.  The Mastermind state lasts 20s and will be cleared after Yelan fires 5 arrows.
