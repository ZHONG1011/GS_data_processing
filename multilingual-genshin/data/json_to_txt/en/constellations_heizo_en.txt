Named Juvenile Casebook
For 5s after Shikanoin Heizou takes the field, his Normal Attack SPD is increased by 15%. He also gains 1 Declension stack for <color=#FFD780FF>Heartstopper Strike</color>. This effect can be triggered once every 10s.
Investigative Collection
The pull effect of the Arresting Windtunnel created by <color=#FFD780FF>Windmuster Kick</color> is enhanced, and its duration is increased to 1s.
Esoteric Puzzle Book
Increases the Level of <color=#FFD780FF>Heartstopper Strike</color> by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Tome of Lies
The first Windmuster Iris explosion in each <color=#FFD780FF>Windmuster Kick</color> will regenerate 9 Elemental Energy for Shikanoin Heizou. Every subsequent explosion in that Windmuster Kick will each regenerate an additional 1.5 Energy for Heizou. One <color=#FFD780FF>Windmuster Kick</color> can regenerate a total of 13.5 Energy for Heizou in this manner.
Secret Archive
Increases the Level of <color=#FFD780FF>Windmuster Kick</color> by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Curious Casefiles
Each Declension stack will increase the CRIT Rate of the <color=#FFD780FF>Heartstopper Strike</color> unleashed by 4%. When Heizou possesses Conviction, this Heartstopper Strike's CRIT DMG is increased by 32%.
