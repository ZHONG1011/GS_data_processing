Conviction
Diluc deals 15% more DMG to opponents whose HP is above 50%.
Searing Ember
When Diluc takes DMG, his ATK increases by 10% and his ATK SPD increases by 5%. Lasts for 10s. This effect can stack up to 3 times and can only occur once every 1.5s.
Fire and Steel
Increases the Level of <color=#FFD780FF>Searing Onslaught</color> by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Flowing Flame
Casting <color=#FFD780FF>Searing Onslaught</color> in rhythm greatly increases damage dealt. 2s after casting <color=#FFD780FF>Searing Onslaught</color>, casting the next Searing Onslaught in the combo deals 40% additional DMG. This effect lasts for 2s.
Phoenix, Harbinger of Dawn
Increases the Level of <color=#FFD780FF>Dawn</color> by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Flaming Sword, Nemesis of the Dark
After casting <color=#FFD780FF>Searing Onslaught</color>, the next 2 Normal Attacks within the next 6s will have their DMG and ATK SPD increased by 30%. Additionally, Searing Onslaught will not interrupt the Normal Attack combo.
