Increases the Level of <color=#FFD780FF>Dance of Abzendegi: Distant Dreams, Listening Spring</color> by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Fricative Pulse
After the third dance step of <color=#FFD780FF>Dance of Haftkarsvar</color>'s Pirouette hits opponents, Nilou will gain 15 Elemental Energy, and DMG from her <color=#FFD780FF>Dance of Abzendegi: Distant Dreams, Listening Spring</color> will be increased by 50% for 8s.
Twirling Light
Increases the Level of <color=#FFD780FF>Dance of Haftkarsvar</color> by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Frostbreaker's Melody
For every 1,000 points of Max HP, Nilou's CRIT Rate and CRIT DMG will increase by 0.6% and 1.2% respectively. The maximum increase in CRIT Rate and CRIT DMG via this method is 30% and 60% respectively.
