Normal Attack: Ceremonial Bladework
<color=#FFD780FF>Normal Attack</color> Performs up to 5 rapid strikes.  <color=#FFD780FF>Charged Attack</color> Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to unleash 2 rapid sword strikes.  <color=#FFD780FF>Plunging Attack</color> Plunges from mid-air to strike the ground below, damaging opponents along the path and dealing AoE DMG upon impact.
Frostgnaw
Unleashes a frigid blast, dealing <color=#99FFFFFF>Cryo DMG</color> to opponents in front of Kaeya.  <i>Kaeya seems affable, but his nature is that of unyielding ice.</i>
Glacial Waltz
Coalescing the frost in the air, Kaeya summons 3 icicles that revolve around him. These icicles will follow the character around and deal <color=#99FFFFFF>Cryo DMG</color> to opponents in their path for the ability's duration.  <i>A winsome smile, genial words, and the merciless hoarfrost that lies beneath... These are all Kaeya's weapons.</i>
Cold-Blooded Strike
Every hit with <color=#FFD780FF>Frostgnaw</color> regenerates HP for Kaeya equal to 15% of his ATK.
Glacial Heart
Opponents <color=#99FFFFFF>Frozen</color> by <color=#FFD780FF>Frostgnaw</color> will drop additional Elemental Particles. Frostgnaw may only produce a maximum of 2 additional Elemental Particles per use.
Hidden Strength
Decreases sprinting Stamina consumption for your own party members by 20%. Not stackable with Passive Talents that provide the exact same effects.
