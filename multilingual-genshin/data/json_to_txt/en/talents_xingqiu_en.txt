Normal Attack: Guhua Style
<color=#FFD780FF>Normal Attack</color> Performs up to 5 rapid strikes.  <color=#FFD780FF>Charged Attack</color> Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to unleash 2 rapid sword strikes.  <color=#FFD780FF>Plunging Attack</color> Plunges from mid-air to strike the ground below, damaging opponents along the path and dealing AoE DMG upon impact.
Guhua Sword: Fatal Rainscreen
Xingqiu performs twin strikes with his sword, dealing <color=#80C0FFFF>Hydro DMG</color>. At the same time, this ability creates the maximum number of Rain Swords, which will orbit your active character. The Rain Swords have the following properties: 路When a character takes DMG, the Rain Sword will shatter, reducing the amount of DMG taken. 路Increases the character's resistance to interruption. 20% of Xingqiu's <color=#80C0FFFF>Hydro DMG Bonus</color> will be converted to additional DMG Reduction for the Rain Swords.  The maximum amount of additional DMG Reduction that can be gained this way is 24%. The initial maximum number of Rain Swords is 3. Using this ability applies the <color=#80C0FFFF>Wet</color> status onto the character.  <i>"Easier to slay a rain dragon than to sever the rain."</i>
Guhua Sword: Raincutter
Initiate Rainbow Bladework and fight using an illusory sword rain, while creating the maximum number of Rain Swords.  <color=#FFD780FF>Rainbow Bladework</color> 路Your active character's Normal Attacks will trigger consecutive sword rain attacks, dealing <color=#80C0FFFF>Hydro DMG</color>. 路Rain Swords will remain at the maximum number throughout the ability's duration.  <i>The "Way of the Rain Cutter" is one of the esoteric secret techniques of the Guhua Sect.</i>
Hydropathic
When a Rain Sword is shattered or when its duration expires, it regenerates the current character's HP based on 6% of Xingqiu's Max HP.
Blades Amidst Raindrops
Xingqiu gains a 20% <color=#80C0FFFF>Hydro DMG Bonus</color>.
Flash of Genius
