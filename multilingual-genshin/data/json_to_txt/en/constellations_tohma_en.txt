A Comrade's Duty
When a character protected by Thoma's own <color=#FFD780FF>Blazing Barrier</color> (Thoma excluded) is attacked, Thoma's own <color=#FFD780FF>Blazing Blessing</color> CD is decreased by 3s, while his own <color=#FFD780FF>Crimson Ooyoroi</color>'s CD is decreased by 3s. This effect can be triggered once every 20s.
A Subordinate's Skills
<color=#FFD780FF>Crimson Ooyoroi</color>'s duration is increased by 3s.
Fortified Resolve
Increases the Level of <color=#FFD780FF>Blazing Blessing</color> by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Long-Term Planning
After using <color=#FFD780FF>Crimson Ooyoroi</color>, 15 Energy will be restored to Thoma.
Raging Wildfire
Increases the Level of <color=#FFD780FF>Crimson Ooyoroi</color> by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Burning Heart
When a <color=#FFD780FF>Blazing Barrier</color> is obtained or refreshed, the DMG dealt by all party members' Normal, Charged, and Plunging Attacks is increased by 15% for 6s.
