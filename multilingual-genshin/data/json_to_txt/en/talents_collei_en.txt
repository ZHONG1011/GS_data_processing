Normal Attack: Supplicant's Bowmanship
<color=#FFD780FF>Normal Attack</color> Performs up to 4 consecutive shots with a bow.  <color=#FFD780FF>Charged Attack</color> Performs a more precise <color=#FFD780FF>Aimed Shot</color> with increased DMG. While aiming, Dendro energy will accumulate on the arrowhead. A fully charged arrow will deal <color=#99FF88FF>Dendro DMG</color>.  <color=#FFD780FF>Plunging Attack</color> Fires off a shower of arrows in mid-air before falling and striking the ground, dealing AoE DMG upon impact.
Floral Brush
Throws out a Floral Ring that deals 1 instance of <color=#99FF88FF>Dendro DMG</color> to targets it comes into contact with. The Floral Ring will return after a set time, dealing <color=#99FF88FF>Dendro DMG</color> once again.  <i>This is Collei's go-to gadget for dealing with unruly wild beasts. A few good thwacks from this tool will generally improve their behavior. This same principle works just as well on evildoers.</i>
Trump-Card Kitty
Trusty Cuilein-Anbar comes to save the day! Throws the doll named Cuilein-Anbar, causing an explosion that deals <color=#99FF88FF>AoE Dendro DMG</color>, creating a Cuilein-Anbar Zone. Cuilein-Anbar will bounce around within this zone, dealing <color=#99FF88FF>AoE Dendro DMG</color>.  <i>"Pretty impressive, huh? ... Eh? You want to know how I came up with the name Cuilein-Anbar? Uh, well, I..."</i>
Floral Sidewinder
If one of your party members has triggered Burning, Quicken, Aggravate, Spread, Bloom, Hyperbloom, or Burgeon reactions before the <color=#FFD780FF>Floral Ring</color> returns, it will grant the character the Sprout effect upon return, which will continuously deal <color=#99FF88FF>Dendro DMG</color> equivalent to 40% of Collei's ATK to nearby opponents for 3s. If another Sprout effect is triggered during its initial duration, the initial effect will be removed. DMG dealt by Sprout is considered Elemental Skill DMG.
The Languid Wood
When a character within the <color=#FFD780FF>Cuilein-Anbar Zone</color> triggers Burning, Quicken, Aggravate, Spread, Bloom, Hyperbloom, or Burgeon reactions, the Zone's duration will be increased by 1s. A single <color=#FFD780FF>Trump-Card Kitty</color> can be extended by up to 3s.
Gliding Champion of Sumeru
Decreases gliding Stamina consumption for your own party members by 20%. Not stackable with Passive Talents that provide the exact same effects.
