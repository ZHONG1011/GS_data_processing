Gaze of the Deep
Even when Oz is not present in combat, he can still watch over Fischl through his raven eyes. When Fischl performs a Normal Attack against an opponent, Oz fires a joint attack, dealing DMG equal to 22% of Fischl's ATK.
Devourer of All Sins
When <color=#FFD780FF>Nightrider</color> is used, it deals an additional 200% ATK as DMG, and its AoE is increased by 50%.
Wings of Nightmare
Increases the Level of <color=#FFD780FF>Nightrider</color> by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Her Pilgrimage of Bleak
When <color=#FFD780FF>Midnight Phantasmagoria</color> is used, it deals 222% of ATK as <color=#FFACFFFF>Electro DMG</color> to surrounding opponents. When the skill duration ends, Fischl regenerates 20% of her HP.
Against the Fleeing Light
Increases the Level of <color=#FFD780FF>Midnight Phantasmagoria</color> by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Evernight Raven
Extends the duration of Oz's presence on the field by 2s. Additionally, Oz performs joint attacks with your active character when present, dealing 30% of Fischl's ATK as <color=#FFACFFFF>Electro DMG</color>.
