Normal Attack: Favonius Bladework
<color=#FFD780FF>Normal Attack</color> Performs up to 5 consecutive strikes.  <color=#FFD780FF>Charged Attack</color> Consumes a certain amount of stamina to launch an opponent using the power of wind. Launched opponents will slowly fall to the ground.  <color=#FFD780FF>Plunging Attack</color> Plunges from mid-air to strike the ground below, damaging opponents along the path and dealing AoE DMG upon impact.
Gale Blade
Focusing the might of the formless wind around her blade, Jean releases a miniature storm, launching opponents in the direction she aims at, dealing massive <color=#80FFD7FF>Anemo DMG</color>.  <color=#FFD780FF>Hold</color> At the cost of continued stamina consumption, Jean can command the whirlwind to pull surrounding opponents and objects towards her front. Direction can be adjusted. Character is immobile during skill duration.  <i>That which follows Jean, the wholehearted defender, is a guardian wind that can banish danger from her comrades' presence.</i>
Dandelion Breeze
Calling upon the wind's protection, Jean creates a swirling Dandelion Field, launching surrounding opponents and dealing <color=#80FFD7FF>Anemo DMG</color>. At the same time, she instantly regenerates a large amount of HP for all party members. The amount of HP restored scales off Jean's ATK.  <color=#FFD780FF>Dandelion Field</color> ·Continuously regenerates HP of characters within the AoE and continuously imbues them with <color=#80FFD7FF>Anemo</color>. ·Deals <color=#80FFD7FF>Anemo DMG</color> to opponents entering or exiting the Dandelion Field.  <i>Jean's magnanimity is as vast as the dandelion fields, and it is the root of her wish to protect others.</i>
Wind Companion
On hit, Jean's Normal Attacks have a 50% chance to regenerate HP equal to 15% of Jean's ATK for all party members.
Let the Wind Lead
Using <color=#FFD780FF>Dandelion Breeze</color> will regenerate 20% of its Energy.
Guiding Breeze
When a Perfect Cooking is achieved on a dish with restorative effects, there is a 12% chance to obtain double the product.
