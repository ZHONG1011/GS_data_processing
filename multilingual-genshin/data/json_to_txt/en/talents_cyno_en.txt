Normal Attack: Invoker's Spear
<color=#FFD780FF>Normal Attack</color> Performs up to 4 consecutive spear strikes.  <color=#FFD780FF>Charged Attack</color> Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to lunge forward, dealing damage to opponents along the way.  <color=#FFD780FF>Plunging Attack</color> Plunges from mid-air to strike the ground below, damaging opponents along the path and dealing AoE DMG upon impact.
Secret Rite: Chasmic Soulfarer
Performs a swift thrust, dealing <color=#FFACFFFF>Electro DMG</color> to opponents along the path.  When Cyno is under the Pactsworn Pathclearer state triggered by Sacred Rite: Wolf's Swiftness, he will instead unleash a <color=#FFD780FF>Mortuary Rite</color> that deals thunderous <color=#FFACFFFF>AoE Electro DMG</color> and extends the duration of Pactsworn Pathclearer.  <i>"This judgment brooks no choice. There is no rejection or acceptance here... We have both long been bound up in this conviction."</i>
Sacred Rite: Wolf's Swiftness
Calls upon a divine spirit to indwell him, morphing into the Pactsworn Pathclearer.  <color=#FFD780FF>Pactsworn Pathclearer</color> Cyno's Normal, Charged, and Plunging Attacks will be converted to <color=#FFACFFFF>Electro DMG</color> that cannot be overridden. ·Cyno's Elemental Mastery and resistance to interruption will increase, and he gains immunity to Electro-Charged DMG.  This effect will be canceled when Cyno leaves the field and lasts a maximum of 18s.  <i>The secret rituals passed down within the ancient nation are as a final judgment to the General Mahamatra.</i> <i>Whether now or in the past, whether great storm or serpent, none shall shake the majesty of the law.</i>
Featherfall Judgment
When Cyno is in the Pactsworn Pathclearer state activated by <color=#FFD780FF>Sacred Rite: Wolf's Swiftness</color>, Cyno will enter the Endseer stance at intervals. If he activates <color=#FFD780FF>Secret Rite: Chasmic Soulfarer</color> while affected by this stance, he will activate the Judication effect, increasing the DMG of this Secret Rite: Chasmic Soulfarer by 35%, and firing off 3 Duststalker Bolts that deal 100% of Cyno's ATK as <color=#FFACFFFF>Electro DMG.</color> Duststalker Bolt DMG is considered Elemental Skill DMG.
Authority Over the Nine Bows
Cyno's DMG values will be increased based on his Elemental Mastery as follows: ·Pactsworn Pathclearer's Normal Attack DMG is increased by 150% of his Elemental Mastery. ·Duststalker Bolt DMG from his Passive Talent Featherfall Judgment is increased by 250% of his Elemental Mastery.
The Gift of Silence
Gains 25% more rewards when dispatched on a Sumeru Expedition for 20 hours.
