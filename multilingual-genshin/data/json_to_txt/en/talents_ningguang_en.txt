Normal Attack: Sparkling Scatter
<color=#FFD780FF>Normal Attack</color> Shoots gems that deal <color=#FFE699FF>Geo DMG</color>. Upon hit, this grants Ningguang 1 Star Jade.  <color=#FFD780FF>Charged Attack</color> Consumes a certain amount of stamina to fire off a giant gem that deals <color=#FFE699FF>Geo DMG</color>. If Ningguang has any Star Jades, unleashing a Charged Attack will cause the Star Jades to be fired at the enemy as well, dealing additional DMG.  <color=#FFD780FF>Plunging Attack</color> Gathering the might of Geo, Ningguang plunges towards the ground from mid-air, damaging all opponents in her path. Deals <color=#FFE699FF>AoE Geo DMG</color> upon impact with the ground.
Jade Screen
Ningguang creates a Jade Screen out of gold, obsidian and her great opulence, dealing <color=#FFE699FF>AoE Geo DMG</color>.  <color=#FFD780FF>Jade Screen</color> ·Blocks opponents' projectiles. ·Endurance scales based on Ningguang's Max HP.  Jade Screen is considered a <color=#FFE699FF>Geo Construct</color> and can be used to block certain attacks, but cannot be climbed. Only one Jade Screen may exist at any one time.  <i>The canvas of stars is written upon this jade screen.</i>
Starshatter
Gathering a great number of gems, Ningguang scatters them all at once, sending homing projectiles at her opponents that deal massive <color=#FFE699FF>Geo DMG</color>. If Starshatter is cast when a Jade Screen is nearby, the Jade Screen will fire additional gem projectiles at the same time.  <i>Stars shatter. Silence falls.</i>
Backup Plan
When Ningguang is in possession of Star Jades, her Charged Attack does not consume Stamina.
Strategic Reserve
A character that passes through the <color=#FFD780FF>Jade Screen</color> will gain a 12% <color=#FFE699FF>Geo DMG Bonus</color> for 10s.
Trove of Marvelous Treasures
Displays the location of nearby <color=#FFD780FF>ore veins used in forging</color> on the mini-map.
