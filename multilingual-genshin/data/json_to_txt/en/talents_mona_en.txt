Normal Attack: Ripple of Fate
<color=#FFD780FF>Normal Attack</color> Performs up to 4 water splash attacks that deal <color=#80C0FFFF>Hydro DMG</color>.  <color=#FFD780FF>Charged Attack</color> Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to deal <color=#80C0FFFF>AoE Hydro DMG</color> after a short casting time.  <color=#FFD780FF>Plunging Attack</color> Gathering the might of Hydro, Mona plunges towards the ground from mid-air, damaging all opponents in her path. Deals <color=#80C0FFFF>AoE Hydro DMG</color> upon impact with the ground.
Mirror Reflection of Doom
Creates an illusory Phantom of Fate from coalesced waterspouts.  <color=#FFD780FF>Phantom</color> Has the following special properties: ·Continuously taunts nearby opponents, attracting their fire. ·Continuously deals <color=#80C0FFFF>Hydro DMG</color> to nearby opponents. ·When its duration expires, the Phantom explodes, dealing <color=#80C0FFFF>AoE Hydro DMG</color>.  <color=#FFD780FF>Hold</color> Utilizes water currents to move backwards swiftly before conjuring a Phantom.  Only one Phantom created by Mirror Reflection of Doom can exist at any time.  <i>Skilled astrologers have always dreamed of using a phantom facsimile of themselves to ward off imminent ill fate, but Mona must be the first to make such a mockery of it in all of Teyvat.</i>
Illusory Torrent
<color=#FFD780FF>Alternate Sprint</color> Mona cloaks herself within the water's flow, consuming Stamina to move rapidly.  When under the effect of Illusory Torrent, Mona can move at high speed on water. Applies the <color=#80C0FFFF>Wet</color> status to nearby opponents when she reappears.  <i>This is something that Mona can do, but her teacher cannot. Well, but it's not as if her teacher would pursue any matter concerning Hydro Visions anyway...</i>
Stellaris Phantasm
Mona summons the sparkling waves and creates a reflection of the starry sky, applying the Illusory Bubble status to opponents in a large AoE.  <color=#FFD780FF>Illusory Bubble</color> Traps opponents inside a pocket of destiny and also makes them <color=#80C0FFFF>Wet</color>. Renders weaker opponents immobile. When an opponent affected by Illusory Bubble sustains DMG, it has the following effects: ·Applies an Omen to the opponent, which gives a DMG Bonus, also increasing the DMG of the attack that causes it. ·Removes the Illusory Bubble, dealing <color=#80C0FFFF>Hydro</color> DMG in the process.  <color=#FFD780FF>Omen</color> During its duration, increases DMG taken by opponents.  <i>It was from the time that gods first traced the lights in the heavens with their eyes that the notion of "fate" was born.</i>
"Come 'n' Get Me, Hag!"
After she has used <color=#FFD780FF>Illusory Torrent</color> for 2s, if there are any opponents nearby, Mona will automatically create a Phantom. A Phantom created in this manner lasts for 2s, and its explosion DMG is equal to 50% of <color=#FFD780FF>Mirror Reflection of Doom</color>.
Waterborne Destiny
Increases Mona's <color=#80C0FFFF>Hydro DMG Bonus</color> by a degree equivalent to 20% of her Energy Recharge rate.
Principium of Astrology
When Mona crafts Weapon Ascension Materials, she has a 25% chance to refund a portion of the crafting materials used.
