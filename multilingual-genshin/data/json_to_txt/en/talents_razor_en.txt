Normal Attack: Steel Fang
<color=#FFD780FF>Normal Attack</color> Performs up to 4 consecutive strikes.  <color=#FFD780FF>Charged Attack</color> Drains Stamina over time to perform continuous spinning attacks against all nearby opponents. At the end of the sequence, performs a more powerful slash.   <color=#FFD780FF>Plunging Attack</color> Plunges from mid-air to strike the ground below, damaging opponents along the path and dealing AoE DMG upon impact.
Claw and Thunder
#Hunts his prey using the techniques taught to him by his master and his lupical.  <color=#FFD780FF>{LAYOUT_MOBILE#Tap}{LAYOUT_PC#Press}{LAYOUT_PS#Press}</color> Swings the Thunder Wolf Claw, dealing <color=#FFACFFFF>Electro DMG</color> to opponents in front of Razor. Upon striking an opponent, Razor will gain an Electro Sigil, which increases his Energy Recharge rate. Razor can have up to 3 Electro Sigils simultaneously, and gaining a new Electro Sigil refreshes their duration.  <color=#FFD780FF>Hold</color> Gathers Electro energy to unleash a lightning storm over a small AoE, causing massive <color=#FFACFFFF>Electro DMG</color>, and clears all of Razor's Electro Sigils. Each Electro Sigil cleared in this manner will be converted into Energy for Razor.  <i>In the moment when lightning strikes, you can sometimes glimpse the look of a predator sizing up his prey deep within Razor's eyes.</i>
Lightning Fang
Summons the Wolf Within, which deals <color=#FFACFFFF>Electro DMG</color> to all nearby opponents. This clears all of Razor's Electro Sigils, which will be converted into Elemental Energy for him. The Wolf Within will fight alongside Razor for the skill's duration.  <color=#FFD780FF>The Wolf Within</color> ·Strikes alongside Razor's normal attacks, dealing <color=#FFACFFFF>Electro DMG</color>. ·Raises Razor's ATK SPD and <color=#FFACFFFF>Electro RES</color>. ·Causes Razor to be immune to DMG inflicted by the <color=#FFACFFFF>Electro-Charged</color> status. ·Disables Razor's Charged Attacks. ·Increases Razor's resistance to interruption.  These effects end when Razor leaves the battlefield. When Razor leaves the field, a maximum of 10 Energy will be returned to him based off the duration remaining on this skill.  <i>The wolf has awoken. The hunt is on.</i>
Awakening
Decreases <color=#FFD780FF>Claw and Thunder</color>'s CD by 18%. Using <color=#FFD780FF>Lightning Fang</color> resets the CD of <color=#FFD780FF>Claw and Thunder</color>.
Hunger
When Razor's Energy is below 50%, increases Energy Recharge by 30%.
Wolvensprint
