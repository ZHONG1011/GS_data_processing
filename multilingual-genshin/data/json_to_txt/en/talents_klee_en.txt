Normal Attack: Kaboom!
<color=#FFD780FF>Normal Attack</color> Throws things that go boom when they hit things! Performs up to 3 explosive attacks, dealing <color=#FF9999FF>AoE Pyro DMG</color>.  <color=#FFD780FF>Charged Attack</color> Consumes a certain amount of Stamina and deals <color=#FF9999FF>AoE Pyro DMG</color> to opponents after a short casting time.  <color=#FFD780FF>Plunging Attack</color> Gathering the power of Pyro, Klee plunges towards the ground from mid-air, damaging all opponents in her path. Deals <color=#FF9999FF>AoE Pyro DMG</color> upon impact with the ground.
Jumpy Dumpty
Jumpy Dumpty is tons of boom-bang-fun!  When thrown, Jumpy Dumpty bounces thrice, igniting and dealing <color=#FF9999FF>AoE Pyro DMG</color> with every bounce.  On the third bounce, the bomb splits into many mines. The mines will explode upon contact with opponents, or after a short period of time, dealing <color=#FF9999FF>AoE Pyro DMG</color>.  Starts with 2 charges.  <i>Jumpy Dumpty is Klee's good friend! She can't help but introduce him to everyone.</i>
Sparks 'n' Splash
Klee's Blazing Delight! For the duration of this ability, continuously summons Sparks 'n' Splash to attack nearby opponents, dealing <color=#FF9999FF>AoE Pyro DMG</color>.  <i>The knights all believe that Klee must have a lucky star watching over her to have never been hurt by any of her own bombs...</i>
Pounding Surprise
When <color=#FFD780FF>Jumpy Dumpty</color> and Normal Attacks deal DMG, Klee has a 50% chance to obtain an Explosive Spark. This Explosive Spark is consumed by the next Charged Attack, which costs no Stamina and deals 50% increased DMG.
Sparkling Burst
When Klee's Charged Attack results in a CRIT Hit, all party members gain 2 Elemental Energy.
All Of My Treasures!
Displays the location of nearby <color=#FFD780FF>resources unique to Mondstadt</color> on the mini-map.
