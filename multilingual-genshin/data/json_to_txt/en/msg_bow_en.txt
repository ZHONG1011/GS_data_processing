Messenger
A basic wooden bow. It is said to have once been used as a tool for long-distance communication.
 It was said in days gone by, The people of Liyue passed messages by bow and arrow. "Wouldn't bystanders get hurt, you ask?" "Suffice to say, there's a good reason this bow is an antique..." The antique shop owner rubbed their chin and smiled.
