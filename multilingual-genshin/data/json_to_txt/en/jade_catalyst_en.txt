Emerald Orb
A catalyst carved out of the hard jade from Jueyun Karst north of Liyue. Small, light, and durable, it is known more colloquially as the "jade ball."
 A lightweight catalyst carved out of emerald-green jade. It is durable and affordable, which makes it a popular item all around. Small but exquisitely made, it is colloquially referred to by the people of Liyue as the "jade ball." It is a highly efficient defensive Catalyst as well as a good-looking accessory.
